Frequently Asked Questions

Go to the Sign In page and click "Forgot password?". We'll send a reset link to the email address associated with your account. The link expires after 30 minutes for security reasons.

You can request a refund within 14 days of purchase, as long as the game has been played for less than 2 hours. Visit your Order History and select "Request Refund" next to the eligible item.

First, verify the integrity of the game files from your Library. Update your graphics drivers, and make sure your system meets the minimum requirements. If the issue persists, submit a support ticket with your crash log attached.

By default, games are installed to your system drive under GameHub's install folder. You can change the default install location from Settings before downloading a new game.

Account merging isn't supported automatically. Please submit a support ticket with both account usernames and we'll manually review your request.

We accept major credit/debit cards, e-wallets, and bank transfers depending on your region. Available options are shown automatically at checkout.
